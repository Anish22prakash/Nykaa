FAQ

  1. What is the offer?

    • Flat 10% off on a minimum spend of Rs 3000 on Citibank debit and credit cards.

    • Maximum discount of Rs. 500 on CITI Credit and Debit card transactions.

    • Offer valid once per device, per card and per user.

    • Offer valid only on the Nykaa Beauty App, Mobile site using the code NYKCITIBANK10

    • Offer not valid on Nykaa Retail Stores.

  2. How can I avail the offer?

    • The Offer is valid from 3 May 2022 to 31 May 2022 (both days inclusive).

    • Download Nykaa Beauty App, Visit the Mobile site > Add products to cart > Click on checkout > Click on proceed to pay and punch the voucher code NYKHSBC > Select Credit/Debit card option and complete the payment..

  3. Can I place multiple orders and avail the discount?

    • No, the offer will be applicable only once per card and per user for one transaction. It cannot be applied on multiple orders.

    • Additionally, in case the order, where a bank offer is applied, is cancelled for any reasons whatsoever, the offer cannot be availed again on the same card.

  4. Can I place multiple orders and avail the discount?

    • Partial cancellation is not allowed for these orders. Full order cancellation can be done. In this case the entire paid amount will be refunded to your HSBC Credit or Debit Card.

    • In case of returns/ cancellations, the refund will be net of the discount availed

    • In case of full order cancellation, the offer cannot be availed again on the given card.

  5. Can I use NykaaBeautyreward points in the orders with HSBC bank offer?

    • Yes, reward points can be redeemed in the same order.

  6. Can I use Nykaa Beauty Gift cards and Nykaa Beauty wallet along with the HSBC card to pay for the order?

    • For all orders where HSBC bank offer is being availed via the coupon code, no other payment modes (including Nykaa Beauty Gift Cards and Nykaa Beauty Wallet) will be available.

    • However, for all other orders, you can use Nykaa Beauty Gift Card or Nykaa Beauty Wallet along with your HSBC bank Debit or Credit Card to complete the transaction.
